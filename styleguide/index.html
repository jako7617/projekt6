QuadSAT - Flight Assistant Manual

Project 6 - Examproject


The purpose of this style guide is to shed light on the design elements and the appurtenant codes that have been used in the online manual, "Flight Assistant Manual". It helps provide structure for future designers and/or programmers that intend to understand it's design principles.


“A styleguide is a set of standards, principles and rules every developer or designer should follow in order to improve the digital presence of the product” - Andrei Dorin
